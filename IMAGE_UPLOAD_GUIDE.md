# Image Upload Instructions

To complete the Luxury Minimalist fitness trainer portfolio, please upload the following images to the `assets/` folder:

## Required Images

### 1. `trainer_hero.jpg`
- **Usage**: Main hero section portrait
- **Dimensions**: Vertical/Portrait orientation (4:5 ratio works best)
- **Style Guide**: 
  - Professional fitness trainer portrait
  - Clean, neutral background (off-white, cream, or soft gray)
  - Professional athletic attire
  - Confident but approachable expression
  - Natural lighting
  
### 2. `schedule_preview.jpg`
- **Usage**: "Concept Training Work" section
- **Dimensions**: Landscape/Horizontal (16:9 or 3:2)
- **Style Guide**:
  - Training schedule or workout plan mockup
  - Can be a tablet/device showing a schedule
  - Or a printed document with structured layout
  - Clean typography, professional appearance
  - Minimal color palette matching the website
  
### 3. `workshop_outline.jpg`
- **Usage**: "Concept Training Work" section  
- **Dimensions**: Landscape/Horizontal (16:9 or 3:2)
- **Style Guide**:
  - Workshop outline or curriculum mockup
  - Similar to schedule_preview but showing workshop/course structure
  - Professional document or presentation style
  - Elegant, structured layout

## Color Palette Reference
To ensure your images match the Luxury Minimalist aesthetic:
- **Background**: Soft Off-White (`#F9F7F2`)
- **Text**: Refined Charcoal (`#1A1A1A`)
- **Accent**: Muted Gold (`#C5A059`)

## Image Quality
- High resolution (at least 1920px on the longest side)
- Good lighting and sharp focus
- Professional photography preferred
- Avoid overly saturated or bright colors

Once uploaded to the `assets/` folder, the website will automatically display them.
